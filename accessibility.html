Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Our commitment

Techniq Skin & Beauty is committed to making this website usable for all visitors, including people with disabilities, and to pursuing practical alignment with recognized accessibility guidance such as WCAG.

Measures we take

  • Responsive layouts and readable text sizing.
  • Descriptive page titles and alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Keyboard-accessible links, buttons, forms, dialogs, and close controls.
  • Visible form labels and understandable instructions.
  • Ongoing review of contrast, structure, and interaction behavior.

Known limitations

Some third-party booking, checkout, map, social media, or other external experiences are not fully controlled by us. We continue improving the portions of the public website we control.

Need assistance?

If you have difficulty using the website or need information in another format, call 860-230-4411 or email techniqskinandbeauty@gmail.com. Please include the page, the issue you experienced, and your preferred contact method.
