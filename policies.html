Policies & Customer Care

Clear expectations help every appointment and product order feel simple and secure.

Appointments and cancellations

Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment. Late cancellations and no-shows are subject to a $29.99 fee. Current availability and service durations are shown in the Square booking calendar.

Secure booking and payment

Appointments are booked through Square. Online product checkout and applicable payment processing are also handled through Square’s secure checkout experience. Review your service or order details before completing payment.

Product shipping

Product orders of $49.99 or more qualify for free shipping. Orders below that threshold currently show an estimated $11.99 shipping charge. Final product, shipping, and Connecticut tax totals are displayed before payment.

Product questions, changes, and returns

Please contact Techniq Skin & Beauty before sending back a product or if an order arrives incorrect or damaged. Include your name, order details, and a description of the issue so the order can be reviewed and the available next step confirmed.

Skincare guidance

Product and treatment information is educational and is not a substitute for medical advice. Share known sensitivities and relevant medications during your consultation. For a diagnosed condition or medical concern, consult an appropriate healthcare professional.
