Meet your esthetician

Hi, I’m Shaniqua.

I’m a licensed esthetician and the founder of Techniq Skin & Beauty.

I specialize in acne and hyperpigmentation, helping clients understand what their skin needs and build a routine they can actually stay consistent with.

My approach is personal and results-focused. I take time to listen, look closely at what is happening with your skin, and customize each treatment instead of reaching for a one-size-fits-all solution.

I also want you to understand the “why” behind your care, so you leave feeling confident about what to do at home—not overwhelmed by products or trends.
