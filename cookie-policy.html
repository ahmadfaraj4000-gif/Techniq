Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Overview

This policy explains how Techniq Skin & Beauty uses cookies and similar browser-storage technologies. Read it with our Privacy Policy.

Storage currently used

  • techniqCart remembers products placed in the shopping cart.
  • techniqCookieConsent remembers the cookie-banner choice.
  • techniqSkinResetPromoSeen prevents the new-client popup from appearing repeatedly.

These functions support the experience visitors request. The website does not itself load behavioral advertising cookies. Square booking and checkout pages and other external services may use their own cookies under their policies.

Your choices

The homepage banner lets you accept or decline optional storage. Necessary storage may still be used for features you request, such as maintaining a shopping cart. You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings at any time.

Future changes

If analytics, advertising pixels, retargeting, or other non-essential tracking are added, this policy and the site’s choices will be updated.
