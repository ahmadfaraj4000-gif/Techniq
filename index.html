Frequently Asked Questions

Planning Your Skincare Appointment

Helpful answers before your facial, corrective skincare, or waxing appointment at our Manchester studio serving Vernon and nearby CT clients.

What should I do before my appointment?

Come with a clean face if possible and no heavy makeup. Avoid retinol, exfoliants, or strong actives for 24 to 48 hours before your visit. If you are coming in for waxing, make sure hair is at least 1/4 inch long, about the size of a grain of rice, for best results.

What happens during my first appointment?

Every appointment starts with a skin consultation so I can assess your skin and understand your goals. From there, your treatment is customized to what your skin actually needs that day, not a one-size-fits-all menu service.

How long does an appointment take?

Most facial services run 60 to 90 minutes. Waxing appointments are typically shorter. You will see the estimated time when booking through the calendar.

Will my skin be red or sensitive after?

Some redness or sensitivity is normal, especially after deeper treatments like peels or extractions. It usually fades within a few hours. I will walk you through what to expect and what to avoid after your treatment before you leave.

What should I avoid after my facial?

For 24 to 48 hours after your facial, skip heavy workouts, direct sun exposure, makeup, and active skincare ingredients like retinol or AHAs. Your skin needs time to settle and absorb the benefits of your treatment.

How often should I come in?

For best results, most clients come in every 4 to 6 weeks. Consistency is key. One appointment can refresh your skin, but a regular routine is what creates lasting change.

Do you work with sensitive skin or active acne?

Yes. Treatments are always customized, so whether you are dealing with breakouts, sensitivity, or hyperpigmentation, your service will be adjusted accordingly. If you have specific concerns, you are welcome to reach out before booking.

What is the cancellation policy?

Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations or no-shows are subject to a $30 fee. Life happens, so give as much notice as you can.

How do I book?

Use the Book Now button on the site to view current availability and select your service. The calendar shows real-time openings, so what you see is what is available.
